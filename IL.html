About Collage :
Shahada Taluka Educational Society And Co-Operative Educational Society was eshtablihed in 1952 by great eminent person Dr. Vishramkaka Patil (MSc. USA) to provide educational services to people around Shahada and ultimatly to contribute in development of Shahada block and nearby area. Our society provides educational services from KG to PG at the prime location of Shahada city. Also at the needed places, the sanstha runs Primary Schools, High Schools, and Junior Colleges. Since 1952 , Sanstha has started and runs different primary, high schools and Junior Colleges around Shahada Taluka.
